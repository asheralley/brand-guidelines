Brand Guidelines

Logo

The Logo may be black or white only; this rule applies to all communications materials.

No other color may be used, including grey; it is important to keep this black/white identity seperate from the the adidas Group grey/white identity.

All the elements must appear together as one color (either all black or all white).

Violations
  • _Do not all color to any element of the adidas Brand Logo.
  • _Do not use grey or any other color as a background.
  • _Do not place the adidas Brand Logo over images or graphics.

Layout

Always lay out documents in a 3:1 grid to define logo, image and copy areas.

Portrait: A horizontal grid layout is preferred for portrait formats.

Landscape: Use either a vertical grid or horizontal grid layout.

Imagery: Image myst fill two connected panels of the layout. Full page image is allowed on inside portrait pages only, never on covers.

Violations
  • _Do not alter the 3:1 grid

Colors

Black and white: The adidas Brand colors of black and white are always used for logos, overs and home pages.

Accent colors: We have chosen colors similar to the Olympic Rings as accent colors for the adidas Brand color palette.

Violations
  • _Do not use colors that are not part of the brand palette
